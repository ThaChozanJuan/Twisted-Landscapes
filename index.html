About Twisted Landscapes

Twisted Landscapes is a premier Sydney-based landscaping company dedicated to crafting sustainable, breathtaking outdoor spaces. With over a decade of expertise, our passionate team designs gardens and landscapes that thrive in Sydney’s unique climate.

We specialize in eco-friendly solutions, including native plant gardens, sophisticated hardscaping, and tailored maintenance plans, serving clients across Sydney’s Northern Beaches, Eastern Suburbs, and beyond.
